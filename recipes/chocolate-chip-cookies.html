Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description:

These tasty chocolate chip cookies will be a hit at your next cookout or party they are even good just sitting at home. Made with ingredients found in any kitchen, and easy to make in a pinch.

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 Cups Brown Sugar
  • 3/4 Cups White Sugar
  • 3/4 Cups Shortening
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla
  • 2 1/4 Cups Flour
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Hot Water

Steps to Bake:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 Degrees Farenheight
  2. Mix together the brown sugar, white sugar, and shortening
  3. Add the eggs and vanilla and mix
  4. Sift together the flour, baking soda, and salt, then, mix it with the stuff on the bottom
  5. Add 1 teaspoon of hot water and mix
  6. Bake for 7-9 minutes and then enjoy :)
